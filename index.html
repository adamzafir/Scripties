Frequently Asked Questions

Is Scripties free?

Yes! Scripties is free to download and use. We believe everyone deserves access to professional speech coaching tools.

Does it work offline?

Yes, all features work without an internet connection. Your rehearsals are private and local.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your scripts and recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not use your voice data to train public AI models.
